Community Guidelines

Introduction

While technology helps ensure the speed, dependability, and safety of our service, people matter most to the experience.

Professionalism & Respect

Zero tolerance policy for discrimination, harassment, or abuse.

Non-discrimination

Refusal of service based on race, religion, gender, age, or any protected category is prohibited. Violations may lead to permanent ban.

No inappropriate behavior

Derogatory remarks, personal comments, unwanted contact, or harassment are strictly prohibited.

Compliance with the law

Illegal items and misuse of substances are strictly prohibited. Violations may lead to account suspension.

Disabilities

Compliance with all laws regarding accessibility and delivery is mandatory.

Following the rules

Users must maintain valid documents and follow all platform policies.

Emergencies

In emergencies, contact local authorities first, then inform support.
