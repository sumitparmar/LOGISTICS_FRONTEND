Refund and Cancellation Policy

Overview

Our focus is a complete customer satisfaction. In the event, if you are displeased with the services provided, we will refund back the money, provided the reasons are genuine and proved after investigation.

Please read the fine prints of each deal before buying it, it provides all the details about the services you purchase.

In case of dissatisfaction from our services, clients have the liberty to cancel their order and request a refund.

Cancellation Policy

Customer can cancel the order at any time before the order has been initiated or completed by the courier.

Initiation of the order is defined by courier reaching the pick up point. In such cases only partial refund can be provided.

Completion of the order is defined by courier visiting pickup and at least one delivery location. In such instances refund cannot be initiated.

Customers can cancel via app or dashboard. For support-based cancellation, contact via chat during working hours.

Cancellation Fees

If courier reaches pickup location (or within 30 mins prior), cancellation fee equals pickup charges.

For cash payments: charges deducted via credit note.

For non-cash payments: charges deducted directly from balance and reflected in invoice.

Refund Policy

After investigation and approval, refund will be processed within 7 working days either to Borzo balance or bank account.

Liability

Liability applies only if declared value is accurate and security fee (0.85% + GST) is paid.

Maximum liability is ₹50,000. Orders above this require prior written approval.

For disputes, please contact support via chat.
